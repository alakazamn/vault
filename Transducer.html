Transducer

A transducer is a device that converts one type of energy to another. Speakers, microphones, and the ear are all examples of transducers.

According to Huber, transducers usually are the "weakest link" in an audio chain due to the nature of their function-- they cannot perfectly convert one form of energy into another.[1]
