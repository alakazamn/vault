Performing Rights Organization (PRO)-- collects performance royalties and distributes them to rightsholders

soundexchange-- performance royalty for artist+label applies only to non-interactive digital radio

terrestrial radio-- traditional radio

sync-- royalties for music synced to picture

"deal memo"-- draft of contract delivered to artist before official contract

Types

Performance

Performance royalties are paid whenever a song makes it into the air; i.e. live performances, radio, streaming

  • publisher and songwriter
  • includes plays that are recorded

Mechanical

-- the right to produce the song mechanically (i.e. CD, vinyl)

  • publisher and songwriter
  • used to be Harry Fox but they didn't update for digital
  • 2018-- congress created Mechanical Licensing Collective
  • multiple versions of same song= multiple different mech royalties
  • license is compulsory-- anyone can get a mechanical license; you are compelled to allow someone to record BUT they have to pay the mechanical royalty
