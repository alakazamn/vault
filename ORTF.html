ORTF


ORTF is a stereo micing technique that uses two matched cardioid pattern microphones: they are positioned with their capsules facing outwards and their bases 17cm apart, forming a 110° angle.

  • Realistic stereo field
  • Mono compatibility
  • Less room sound due to cardioid pattern

ORTF utilizes both interaural time and volume difference to achieve its stereo effect.
