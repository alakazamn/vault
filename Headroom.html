Headroom

In mixing, headroom is the amount of dynamic range between the normal level of a signal and the point of distortion or clipping. The amount of headroom can vary across devices. In digital settings, the amount of headroom is dependent on bit depth: higher bit depths mean more dynamic range.[1][2]
