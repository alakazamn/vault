Blumlein

Blumlein is a stereo micing technique in which a matched pair of microphones with omnidirectional patterns are placed with the capsules close together (but not touching), at an angle of approximately 90° to each other.

  • Realistic stereo image
  • More ambient / room sound

Similar to the X/Y technique, which uses cardoid mics at an angle of ~90°-135° to each other.
