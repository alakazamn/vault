PRO Assmnt
  1. Answer the following questions about Performing Rights Organizations:
    1. What is a Performing Rights Organization (PRO)?
    • Collects performance royalties and distributes them to rightsholders
  2. Who do PRO’s collect from and how much?
    • Venues
  3. Who do PRO’s collect for and how are royalties distributed?
    • Songwriters/Publishers -- 50-50 split
  4. What is a Music Publishing company and is it important to have one?
    they commercially leverage music of composers
     getting them paid
  5. What happens to my Publishing royalties if I don’t have a publisher?
    • You don't get any of them-- they go away
  6. Why is copyrighting my music important?
    • So you can actually make money and people don't just take your stuff
  7. How many PRO’s are there?
    • 3 in the US-- ASCAP, BMI, and SESAC.
  8. Describe the major differences between ASCAP, BMI, and SESAC.
    • BMI -- JOIN WITH A FEE
      • BMI longer contract
      • BMI costs money
      • BMI has Taylor Swift award
    • ASCAP-- $50 one time fee
    • SESAC-- exclusive, invite-only
  9. What is a license?
    • an agreement that allows someone else other than the copyright holder to reproduce / perform a song
  10. What is a synch license?
    • For broadcast (i.e. syncing to TV or Movie)
  11. Who are the parties involved in negotiating a synch license?
    • publisher / content owner
    • production company
  12. What is a mechanical license?
    • the right to produce the song mechanically (e.g. CD, vinyl)
  13. Who are the parties involved in negotiating a mechanical license?
    • congress: mechanical licensing collective-- compulsory license with fixed rate
      • anyone can get a mechanical license; you are compelled to allow someone to record BUT they have to pay the mechanical royalty
    • licensee
  14. How do PRO's work in foreign territories?
    US publishers don't deal overseas
    • need sub-publishing agreement for overseas-- with somebody who looks at europe, asia, etc-- probably a bunch for different areas; they take a % cut off the top
      • money sent to publishing company; split then 50-50
  15. As both an artist and a publisher, which PRO would you choose and why?
    • BMI -- i am going to win the Taylor Swift award.
