Spaced Pair

Spaced Pair, or A/B is a stereo micing technique in which a matched pair of microphones are placed several feet apart from each other.[1]

A spaced pair setup utilizes both interaural time and volume difference to achieve its stereo effect.[2] As a result, phase issues can often occur, leading to potentially poor mono compability.[3]
